## What Is Alive Right Now

We are in the heart of winter under a Capricorn Full Moon. This is a threshold moment — one that asks for honest reflection rather than forward momentum. Nature is reminding us that growth begins beneath the surface.

The Capricorn Full Moon illuminates foundations, responsibilities, and the structures we have built our lives upon. It asks: what is solid? What needs to be set down? Where are we still carrying something that is not ours?

Mercury Retrograde in Cancer is slowing communication and bringing old feelings to the surface. This is not a time to make major decisions. It is a time to feel. To revisit. To give old emotions the compassion they never received.

Jupiter is preparing to enter Leo — the sign of the heart, of courage, of being seen. This whispers that after the inner work comes the courage to step forward. What is being built in the quiet will make sense when the light returns.
