The Most Important Thing to Know

These patterns kept you connected.
They kept you safe.
They kept you loved — the only way you knew how.

These behaviours are not your identity.
They are not your fault.
They are not permanent.

They are the beginning of your map home.

When the foundation begins to shift, something powerful happens. Self-abandonment softens. Self-trust returns. Self-love emerges naturally. What once felt like your weakness becomes your wisdom. Sensitivity becomes intuition. Control becomes leadership. Protection becomes discernment.

This is the process of becoming whole — not fixing yourself, but learning to hold the full spectrum of who you are.
