Why This Works

Your subconscious replays old beliefs until they are replaced. Journaling from your future self trains your mind and body to believe in a new version of you — and to act in alignment with that identity.

The Practice: Five prompts. Done daily — mornings are best, before distractions kick in. Don't overthink. Let it flow. One line or many — this is about consistency, not perfection.

Every time you show up for yourself, you're building trust — and every repetition brings your future self closer to now.
