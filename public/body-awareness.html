Reconnect to
Your Body

Your body is not a problem to manage. It is a messenger. Every sensation, every ache, every area of tension is trying to tell you something.

"In accepting and acknowledging your emotional body, you allow for healing and expansion on all levels."

— Tracey Gibson

We begin with a body scan to arrive fully into your physical body. Then we explore what each area of tension, heaviness, or sensation may be holding — and what it is trying to communicate.

Body Scan

Find somewhere comfortable. Close your eyes or soften your gaze. Let this recording guide you home. As you scan, notice which areas call your attention.

Grounding breath: Place your hand here. Breathe in slowly through your nose. Let the exhale be twice as long. You do not need to fix anything. Just listen.
Body Question 1 of 3
How does my body
feel right now?
Notice any sensations. Warmth, tension, openness, contraction. Let each feeling guide you to deeper insight.

If your mind wanders, return to your breath. Let it anchor you into presence.

Body Question 2 of 3
What am I avoiding
feeling?
Your mind's job is to protect you. But the body holds what the mind moves past. Is there something present that you have been bypassing?

You do not need to solve it. Simply be willing to see it.

Body Question 3 of 3
What message is my body
trying to communicate
to me?
Trust what arises — a word, an image, a colour, a feeling. Your body knows. Before your thoughts began, a feeling was activated here. Let yourself receive it.

Consciously ask: "How do I feel?"

Practice Complete

You are not your thoughts.
You are the awareness behind them.

Through this practice, you open the door to living more consciously — guided by the wisdom of your body rather than the noise of your mind.

Remember

Before your thoughts began, a feeling was activated in your body. Our natural protection is to jump into thought — but now you have observed it. You asked: "How do I feel?"

That simple act is how you return home.
