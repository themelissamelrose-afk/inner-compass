22×11 Rewiring
Method

A powerful writing process to challenge and clear limiting beliefs while gently introducing new ones that your subconscious can actually accept.

Why This is Different from Affirmations

Saying "I am wealthy" when you feel broke creates inner resistance. But saying "I am open to feeling supported financially" begins the shift with safety and curiosity.

The Method:
Write your chosen belief 22 times per day. After each line, write your gut-level response — without filtering. These responses are your subconscious revealing what's in the way. Your job is simply to witness them with compassion.

Do this for 11 days. By day 11, you may notice subtle shifts — more spaciousness, softer inner dialogue, spontaneous new actions.

Keep the process sacred. Start fresh if interrupted. Let this be your quiet daily devotion.
