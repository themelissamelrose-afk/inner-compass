Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience in breathwork or coaching?

No prior experience is required. This training is designed to take you from the very beginning. The program starts with your own personal healing through Foundations of Self, so by the time you begin facilitating others, you will have lived the process yourself.

When can I start?

You can begin immediately upon enrolment. You will receive instant access to the Aligned Living Portal, the self-guided Foundations of Self program, and all theory modules. The live Facilitator Training commences September 1, 2026.

What if I cannot make a live call?

All live training calls are recorded and made available in the Aligned Living Portal, so you can catch up at a time that works for you. You will also have access to Voxer support throughout the 12 weeks to ask questions between sessions.

Where are the in-person workshops held?

Workshop locations will be confirmed closer to the start date. Please reach out to Melissa directly at melissa@melissamelrose.com for the latest details on location.

Can I complete this training alongside other work or commitments?

Yes. The program is designed with working practitioners in mind. The combination of self-paced online modules, live calls, and scheduled in-person days allows you to build your knowledge and practice without stepping away from your existing life.

What do I receive at the end of the training?

You will receive dual certification as a Breathwork Experience Facilitator and a Soul Connection Coach, lifetime access to the Breathworks Tribe community, the ability to resell the Foundations of Self program, and the opportunity to continue co-facilitating with Melissa.

How is the payment plan structured?

The payment plan is 3 x $1,500 AUD, billed monthly. The total investment is $4,500, the same as paying in full. The first payment is due on the day you enrol.

Still have a question?

Reach out to Melissa directly at melissa@melissamelrose.com and she will get back to you personally.
