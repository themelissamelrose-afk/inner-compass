Part 1 — Reframe
the Challenge

List 1–3 things that have hurt you or felt hard to forgive. Then write the lesson that rose from the heart — not the mind.

The Reframe Practice
Challenge 1 Something that has hurt me or been hard to forgive:
I'm grateful this happened because it taught me...
Challenge 2 (Optional) Another thing that felt hard:
I'm grateful this happened because it taught me...

Let the lesson rise. It doesn't have to be perfect — just honest.

Part 2 — Activate
the Frequency

Write three things you're deeply grateful for today. Then pause, breathe into the feeling, and let it rise in your chest.

Three Gratitudes
01
After writing, close your eyes, breathe into it, and smile gently.
Close your eyes. Breathe into this feeling. Let gratitude rise in your chest.
02
Pause. Feel it fully before moving on.
Breathe. Smile gently. Feel this expanding in your chest.
03
Read all three aloud when you're ready. Smile as you do.
Now read all three aloud. Smile as you say each one.
