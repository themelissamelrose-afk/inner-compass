Deeper Placements
Your Saturn, Chiron and North Node.

These three placements carry the deepest story of your healing journey. Add them if you know them, or skip each one and explore them later. You can always return.

What do these placements mean? +

Saturn is your greatest teacher. It shows where life feels like a test, where you meet restriction and resistance. But it also shows where you build your deepest mastery when you stop running from the lesson.

Chiron is the wounded healer. Its placement shows your deepest wound, the thing that has felt most broken about you. But Chiron also carries your greatest gift because your medicine always grows from your wound.

North Node is your soul's direction in this lifetime. It points toward what you are growing into, the territory that feels unfamiliar but deeply calling. Your South Node (opposite sign) is where you feel comfortable but can keep you stuck.
