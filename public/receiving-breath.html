The Receiving
Breath

As we step into our future selves, our body becomes the vessel that holds the frequency. We can't think our way into expansion — we must feel it, breathe it, and live it.

Final Integration Practice

This closing breath practice is here to help you regulate, return to the now, and anchor the energy of receiving. Let your nervous system learn that it can safely hold joy, love, and peace.

The Pattern: Inhale 4 · Hold 2 · Exhale 8

As you breathe, repeat silently:
"I am safe to receive. I am safe to expand."

With each breath, imagine your body softening — your chest opening — your heart receiving.

Sit or lie somewhere quiet and comfortable. Place one hand on your heart, one on your belly. Stay with this for 5–10 minutes, or until you feel a shift.

Inhale 4 · Hold 2
Exhale 8

Hands on heart and belly. Let your whole body soften. This breath is your permission to receive.
