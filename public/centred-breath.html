The Centred
Breath

This practice is designed to bring you back into your body, return you to presence, and centre your energy. It is simple yet profound — reconnecting the heart and gut through the breath.

Prepare
Find a quiet spot. Sit or lie comfortably.

Close your eyes and bring your attention inward. Notice your breath as it flows naturally. Feel your hands, your feet, the sensations within.

Now place your hands:

🤲
Left hand
Lower abdomen
just below the belly button
🫶
Right hand
Heart centre
middle of your chest

Imagine your breath as energy gently moving between your hands — rising from belly to heart as you inhale, and softening back from heart to belly as you exhale.

Choose your practice
How many breaths would you like today?

Start with 10 if this is new for you. Build to 20 as the practice deepens.

Reflection & Grounding
Return gently.

Sit quietly and tune in to any sensations, emotions, or insights that arose during your breath.

1
Gently wiggle your fingers and toes. Feel your breath and energy move down through your body — into your legs, your feet, and deep into the earth.
2
Visualise roots growing from the soles of your feet, anchoring you into the support of the earth beneath you.
3
Sit with this question: "What do I need to feel nourished and supported today?"
4
And this one: "Is there anything I can do to honour what I have just released or remembered?"
Trust your body's wisdom to guide you back into centre. It always knows the way home.
