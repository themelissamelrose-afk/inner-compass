Grounding Breath
to Reconnect

A full-body practice that anchors you between Heaven and Earth — connecting your Root, your Crown, and your Heart into one coherent field.

This Practice

Stand barefoot on grass if you can, or sit comfortably with your feet flat on the floor. This practice moves through five stages — each one deepening your connection to yourself and the Earth beneath you.

1
Three grounding sighs — settle into your body
2
Root Chakra — grow your roots into the Earth
3
Earth Energy — breathe up from below × 3
4
Soul Star — breathe down through your crown × 3
5
Heart Space — integrate it all

Step 1 — Arrive

Feel your feet on the floor. Let your weight drop down. Take three deep sighs to release what you've been carrying.

—
Feel your feet on the ground. Press begin.

Step 2 — Root

Your Root Chakra is your foundation — your connection to safety, stability, and the Earth. Activate it now.

Visualisation
Close your eyes. Feel the base of your spine. From your Root Chakra, visualise roots growing downward — down through your feet, through the floor, deep into the Earth.

These roots travel all the way to the Earth Star — a glowing orb of golden earth energy beneath you. Feel yourself becoming heavier. Supported. Held.
While you visualise

Take slow, natural breaths. Let each exhale release you a little deeper into the Earth. Stay with this for as long as feels right.

Step 3 — Earth Energy

Now draw the Earth's energy up through your roots — up through your feet, through your body, and out through the Crown of your head.

Visualisation
With each inhale, feel warm Earth energy rising up through your roots and into your body — up through the belly, the chest, the throat, and flowering out through your Crown.
—
Press begin for 3 earth breaths

Step 4 — Soul Star

Now connect to the light above you. Your Soul Star — about six inches above your Crown — holds your highest self, your soul's purpose, your divine connection.

Visualisation
With each inhale, draw luminous light down from your Soul Star — through your Crown, down through the centre of your body, and exhale it deep into the Earth. You are the bridge between Heaven and Earth.
—
Press begin for 3 soul breaths

Step 5 — Heart Space

Bring it all home — to the Heart. The meeting point between Earth below and Heaven above. Your true centre.

Hand Position

Place your left hand on your heart. Place your right hand gently on top. Feel the warmth between your palms and your chest.

Three Conscious Breaths
With each breath, feel the full connection — roots deep in the Earth, light pouring in from above, all of it meeting in the Heart.

Let your breath integrate the experience. No effort. Just receive.
—
Hands on heart. Press begin.
✦
Grounded. Connected. Present.

You are the bridge between
Heaven and Earth.

Rest in this activated heart space. Let your breath continue to integrate. You may open your eyes slowly, carrying this fullness with you.

"Earth below me. Heaven above me. Heart within me. I am home."
