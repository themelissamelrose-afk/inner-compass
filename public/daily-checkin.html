Weekly Reflection

Becoming your
own witness

We don't recognise what we've navigated until we stop to look. Take your time with these.

What did you navigate this week that you haven't yet stopped to acknowledge?
It doesn't have to be big. Finishing something. Accepting something. Staying present through a hard moment. Name it here.
Which feeling came up most for you? What might it be pointing you toward?
Feelings are not problems to solve. They are information. What might this one be asking of you?
Where did you show up for yourself this week, even when it felt hard?
Showing up can look like rest. Like saying no. Like feeling something instead of running from it. Where did you do that?
Where are you being called to go a little deeper within yourself in the week ahead?
Not as a criticism. As an invitation. Where does something in you want more attention, more honesty, more care?
