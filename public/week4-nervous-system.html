The Mirror Work Practice

Find a mirror — even a phone camera will do. Look into your own eyes. This is where the nervous system reset begins.

1
Stand or sit where you can see your face clearly. Soften your jaw, your shoulders, your eyes.
2
Look into your own eyes. Not at your appearance — into your eyes. Stay there.
3
Say out loud or silently: "I see you. I am here. I am not going anywhere."
4
Notice what arises — discomfort, emotion, resistance. Let it be here. You do not have to fix it.
5
End with: "I love you. I am safe. I am enough."

The Anchoring Breath

4 counts in · 2 hold · 8 counts out
This breath signals safety to your nervous system.

Your nervous system is not a machine to fix. It is a living, breathing part of you that responds to safety. Every time you do this practice, you are sending it one message: you are safe now.

"I am safe to receive. I am safe to expand."

Return to this breath anytime you feel yourself spinning. It is always here.
