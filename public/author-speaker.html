Speaking Topics

Each talk is shaped to the audience and context. All topics can be delivered as a keynote, workshop, or immersive breathwork experience.

The Nervous System at Work

Why your team is exhausted, reactive, and disconnected, and what stress actually does to performance, creativity, and decision-making. Practical tools your people can use immediately.

The Breath as a Leadership Tool

How conscious breathwork rewires the nervous system, builds emotional intelligence, and creates the inner steadiness that no amount of strategy can replace.

Surviving vs Choosing

The difference between a life driven by old survival patterns and one lived from conscious choice. A talk that meets high-achievers exactly where they are and opens a door they didn't know existed.

The Body Keeps the Score

A psycho-spiritual perspective on why insight alone doesn't create change, and how somatic embodiment and breathwork unlock what therapy and mindfulness leave unfinished.

Women, Boundaries and Wellbeing

How women's physiology, nervous systems, and relational wiring intersect with burnout, over-giving, and self-abandonment. Honest, grounded, and deeply relevant for female audiences.

Live Breathwork Experience

A guided conscious connected breathwork journey for groups. Transformative, safe, and unlike anything most audiences have experienced before. The room changes. Every time.
