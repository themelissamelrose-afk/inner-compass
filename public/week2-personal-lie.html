Week 2 · Foundations of Self

Uncover Your
Personal Lie

The Personal Lie is the subconscious belief that sits beneath every pattern, every loop, every moment of holding yourself back. It lives in the mind and in the body. Today we bring it into the light — and then we release it.

Question 1 of 5
Question 1
Take a breath. Close your eyes for a moment. Now — what is the most negative thought you have about yourself?
Write it exactly as it sounds. No softening, no editing. What is the harshest thing your inner voice says to you?
Question 2
What are you most afraid people will find out about you?
This is often where the Personal Lie hides — in the gap between who you show the world and what you secretly fear is true.
Question 3
When do you first remember feeling this way about yourself?
You do not need a specific memory — even a feeling, an age, or a moment is enough. Let whatever comes, come.
Question 4
How has this belief tried to protect you?
Your Personal Lie was never just cruelty — it was a survival strategy. How did it serve you? What did it try to keep you safe from?
Question 5
If this belief were completely untrue — what would become possible for you?
Let yourself dream here. No limits, no "but." What opens up when the lie dissolves?

The Sacred 6 Breath

You have named the lie. Now we release it from your body.

The Sacred 6 is a connected circular breath — no pause between the inhale and the exhale. Breathe through your mouth. Let each breath be full and complete. Six rounds. That is all.

Find a comfortable position. Place your hands on your heart if you feel called to.

Ready

You did something beautiful today.

You looked the lie in the eye. You traced it back to where it came from. And you breathed it out of your body.

This is not a one-time fix — it is the beginning of a new conversation with yourself. Every time you notice the lie surfacing, you can return to this practice.
