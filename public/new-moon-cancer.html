New Moon in Cancer

Returning Home
to Yourself

"Every new beginning begins with a sacred return."

Find a quiet space. Light a candle. Settle in.

Before You Begin

What You'll Need

Gather these before you settle in. There is no rush.

  • A candle
  • A journal and pen
  • A bowl of water
  • A crystal (optional)
  • A cup of ceremonial cacao or herbal tea
  • A blanket and comfortable space
  • Gentle music or silence

When you are ready, continue.

Opening

Opening the Space

Light your candle.

Take three slow breaths.

Place one hand on your heart and one on your womb or lower belly.

Close your eyes and simply arrive.

Feel the support of the Earth beneath you. Feel the rhythm of your own breath.

Say Aloud

"I open this sacred space with love.

I call myself fully home.

May only that which serves my highest good enter this space.

May I have the courage to see clearly, release lovingly, and receive deeply."

Take a few more breaths. There is nowhere to be but here.

Cancer Season

Reflection

Cancer is the keeper of the heart.

She asks us not what we have achieved, but what we have been carrying.

Sit with each question. Allow whatever arises. There is nothing to fix. Only something to witness.

Where have you abandoned yourself to belong?
Where have you protected yourself instead of trusting life?
Where have you continued to mother everyone else while forgetting the woman within?
Release

The Water Ritual

Place your fingertips into the bowl of water.

Water remembers.
Just as your body remembers.

Whisper into the water everything you are ready to release.

Old fears
Old identities
Old responsibilities
Old stories
Old versions of yourself that have completed their purpose

When you are ready, gently pour the water onto the earth or into your garden as an offering of release.

Breath Practice

Sacred Breath

Inhale for four. Exhale for six. Follow the circle.

Soul Writing

Journal Prompts

Complete these sentences. Write without editing yourself. Let your soul speak.

I am ready to stop carrying …
The part of me that is asking for love today is …
My heart longs for …
Home feels like …
The woman I am becoming chooses …
This moon I lovingly release …
This moon I joyfully welcome …
New Moon Intentions

Planting Intentions

Cancer doesn't ask us to chase. She asks us to nurture.

Write three intentions beginning with I am nurturing, rather than I want.

Feel the difference.

Try "I am nurturing deep trust in myself."
Try "I am nurturing relationships that nourish my nervous system."
Try "I am nurturing a life that feels like home."
Closing

Closing Blessing

Place both hands over your heart. Take one final deep breath.

Say aloud:

"I honour every version of myself that brought me here.

I release what no longer belongs to my future.

I welcome what my soul is ready to receive.

May my life become a reflection of the love I hold within.

May I remember that home has never been a place outside of me.

Home has always been me.

And so it is."

Blow out your candle slowly.

Carry this softness into the days ahead.

The New Moon has been planted.
Now trust what wishes to grow.
