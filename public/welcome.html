Add Inner Compass to your home screen so it opens like an app — no browser, no searching for the link.

iPhone / iPad
  1. Open this page in Safari (not Chrome)
  2. Tap the share icon at the bottom of the screen — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Tap Add in the top right corner
Android
  1. Open this page in Chrome
  2. Tap the three dots in the top right corner
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen or Install App
  4. Tap Add

The Inner Compass icon will appear on your home screen, ready whenever you need it.
