Week 5 · Foundations of Self

Shift from Blame
to Self-Responsibility

Self-responsibility is not self-blame. It is not your fault — but it is in your power. This practice helps you move from the story of what happened to you, into the power of what you do next.

What is the situation or person you find yourself blaming right now?
This could be a person, a circumstance, a past version of yourself. Write it honestly — no filter needed here.
Underneath the blame, what are you actually feeling?
Blame is usually a lid on a deeper emotion. Lift the lid.
What is the one thing — small or large — that is within your power to do differently now?
Not what you should have done. Not what they should do. What can you — right now — choose?
