Nervous System
Check-In

A micro-practice you can return to any time of day — before a meeting, after a hard conversation, or whenever you feel yourself moving too fast. All you need is this moment.

How it works

Three simple steps. Three deep sighs. A return to yourself.

① Three breaths — inhale through the nose, sigh out through the mouth
② Three questions — what is your body telling you right now?
③ One mantra — to anchor you into the present moment

Three Sighs

Close your eyes if you can. Let these three breaths be a permission slip to stop — and simply be here.

—
Press begin to start

Listen to
Your Body

Your body is always communicating. Right now, it has something to tell you. Pause and listen.

Question 1 of 3

How does my body feel right now?

Notice any sensations — warmth, tension, openness, heaviness, lightness. No judgement. Just observe.

Sit with this question. Breathe. When you're ready...

Question 2 of 3

What am I avoiding feeling?

Sometimes the body holds what the mind is too busy to process. Is there a feeling present that you've been moving past?

Let whatever arises be welcome. Then when you're ready...

Question 3 of 3

What message is my body trying to communicate to me?

Let it guide you. Your body knows. Trust what comes — a word, an image, a feeling. It is enough.

If your mind wanders, return to your breath. Let it anchor you into presence.

Your Mantra
"I am safe to be
present in my body."
Say this three times — silently or aloud.
Let each breath carry the words deeper.
"In accepting and acknowledging your emotional body, you allow for healing and expansion on all levels."

— Tracey Gibson

Check-In Complete

You are here.
That is enough.

Carry this awareness with you through your day. Your body is your home — and you just came back to it.

Return to this practice anytime:
Before a hard conversation. After a busy morning.
In the bathroom at work. In your car before walking inside.
Wherever you are — you can always come home to yourself.
